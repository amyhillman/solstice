Project Solstice

A statement
of intent.

Sunbit has already done something most fintech companies never will: built a product people trust at the moment they need it most. That trust is not a starting point we take for granted. It is the foundation everything here is built on.

Solstice is the design and experience program for what comes next.

The 2030 platform is not an upgrade to what Sunbit has. It is a new product — built from first principles, for a customer who is ready for more, and a company ready to offer it. Five pillars: BNPL Card, Cash Advance, Personal Loans, Tiered Membership, and a Super App that brings them together into a single intelligent financial relationship.

The work documented here — principles, prototypes, research — is the experience foundation for that platform. A living record, updated as decisions are made and the product takes shape.

We are at the beginning. The architecture is being defined, the brand is being reimagined, the customer experience is being designed from scratch. That is not a caveat. It is the opportunity.

This is the first chapter of a whole new Sunbit.

Project Solstice · Design Principles

What the experience
must do.

These are the non-negotiables — the experience commitments that should survive every architectural and product decision made between now and launch.

Core principles
01
We are for everyone, and the experience proves it
Everyone deserves their best way to pay. What that looks like depends on where you are: your credit, your moment, your goal. Our commitment to every customer is equal; the path we offer is personal.
02
We earn the right to offer more
We introduce new products only after we've delivered real value, and only when the next option genuinely serves where a customer is in their financial life. Qualifying is a threshold, not a reason.
03
One relationship, not a list of products
A customer with a card, a cash advance, and a membership tier should experience one Sunbit, not three products with a shared login. Our experience reflects the person using it, while our org chart stays invisible.
04
Our technology is invisible, our intelligence is felt
Our architecture makes personalization, immediacy, and proactive guidance possible — and none of that is visible to the customer. What they experience is a product that knows them, anticipates what they need, and never makes them work for it.
05
Customer trust is sacred
Sunbit earns trust at the point of sale, a yes when a customer needs it most. That trust is our origin story and our differentiator. We protect it the same way we earned it: plain language, honest terms, no silent changes.
